Pizza

Description

This is a classic pizza recipe with a homemade tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and a variety of toppings. The dough is made from scratch and allowed to rise for a light and airy crust. Customize your pizza with your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, or bell peppers.

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy and has become a global favorite. It typically consists of a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. The dough is usually made from flour, water, yeast, and salt, and can be hand-tossed or rolled out. The sauce is often made from crushed tomatoes, garlic, and herbs. Common toppings include pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, olives, and bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle fresh mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Add your choice of toppings.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden brown.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
